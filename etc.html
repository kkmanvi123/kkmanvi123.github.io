What I've Been Loving

A glimpse of what I've been into lately—books, music, hiking, etc.

Books

Atomic Habits by James Clear - A transformative read about the power of small habits and how they compound over time. It’s a book that motivated me to make positive changes in my daily routine.

Sapiens by Yuval Noah Harari - A fascinating exploration of human history, offering profound insights into how we evolved and shaped the world we live in today.

Project Hail Mary by Andy Weir - A gripping sci-fi thriller about a lone astronaut tasked with saving humanity. It blends humor with tension and deep thinking, making it a captivating read.

Music

Fleetwood Mac - "Silver Springs" and "Rhiannon" evoke deep emotions, making me feel connected to something timeless.

Durand Jones & The Indications - Songs like "Is It Any Wonder" and "Cruisin’ to the Park" bring warmth and nostalgia with their soulful vibe.

Khruangbin - "What Once Was" calms my mind and helps me focus with its smooth, instrumental sound.

Nature

Hiking: Marin Headlands - A beautiful trail offering panoramic views of the Golden Gate Bridge and the Pacific Ocean. The serenity and beauty here always give me a sense of calm and inspiration.

Hiking: Point Reyes National Seashore - A secluded gem along California’s coastline, perfect for exploring coastal cliffs and watching elephant seals. It’s a peaceful escape into nature.
